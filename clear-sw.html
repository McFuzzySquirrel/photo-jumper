This page will help you clear the old service worker and cache so the ONNX fix can work properly.

Why do I need this?
  1. The old service worker is cached in your browser
  2. It's blocking the ONNX Runtime from loading
  3. We need to clear it and reload the new one
Click the button below to reset everything...
Manual steps (if button doesn't work):
  1. Press F12 to open DevTools
  2. Go to "Application" tab
  3. Click "Service Workers" on left
  4. Click "Unregister" next to photo-jumper
  5. Click "Clear storage" on left
  6. Click "Clear site data" button
  7. Close DevTools and reload page (Ctrl+Shift+R)
